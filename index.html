Overview

This short presentation I gave to the professor for whom me and one other undergraduate were doing research. The project that I was working on in this lab, called the Biomedical Optics Research Group, was to construct a powerbox for four devices known as a PMT's. The PMTs observed photons being emmitted from an object that was being hit with a laser. The PHd student who was in charge of the experiment wanted a better and more compact way to control the voltages going to each of the PMTs. These were our plans that we eventually implemented into a testing model.
